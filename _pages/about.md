Welcome! I received my Ph.D degree from [Department of Computer Science](https://computerscience.exeter.ac.uk/), the [University of Exeter](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/), UK. Prior to that, I worked and studied at the University of Otago, New Zealand, and [Beihang University](https://ev.buaa.edu.cn/), Beijing, China. I received my master degree in Control Science and Engineering, and Bachelor Degree in Mechanical Design, Manufacture and Its Automation.<br />
<br />
My research interests include: 
  * Mobile edge computing 
  * Internet of things
  * Intelligent transportation systems
  * Decision-making strategies for future mobile networks
 <br />
 <br />

I am also an Associate Fellow of UKPSF for teaching and supporting learning in higher education, recognized by Advance HE.
